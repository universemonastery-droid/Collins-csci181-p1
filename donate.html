DONATE ITEMS

Looking To donate Items?

Schedule a Pickup

We currently offer pick ups Monday- Friday in Missoula, or you can drop items off during our regular business hours. Our donation schedule fills up quickly, so we ask for at least a couple days notice but may scheduled out a week in advance during busy times. We occasionally have spots open for Frenchtown, Lolo, Florence and other areas on the outskirts. Please call us if you are outside of these areas to see when we will be there next.


Please see the following list of common items that we can and cannot accept. The following items may be dropped off at our store during business hours, or contact us to arrange a time for pickup.


We cannot guarantee acceptance of any items until we see them in person. You are welcome to send us photos of items and we can give you a better idea if it is a donation we can accept at the time. Our acceptance qualifications may vary throughout the year depending on other inventory and space.


Appliances


We do offer free pickup for working appliances. We are happy to take nonworking appliances, but we ask that you drop them at our store during regular business hours.

  • Refrigerators
  • Stoves
  • Ovens/Ranges
  • Microwaves
  • Washing Machines
  • Dryers
  • Dishwashers
  • Air Conditioners

Furniture


  • Sofas*
  • Chairs* (Living room, office, recliner, ect)
  • Tables (Dining room, side)
  • Dressers
  • Bedframes (no water bed frames, storage headboards or missing pieces)
  • Coffee/End Tables
  • Filing Cabnets
  • Bookshelves
  • Home Decor and Artwork
  • Small computer desks
  • Gaming tables (poker, foosball, airhocky or ping pong)
  • Mattress/Boxsprings (We will only accept items that have no stains, rips, odors, ect. We reserve the right to turn down donation of such items)
  • Some exercise equipment (typically only treadmills & ellipticals)

*Upholstered furniture cannot have any stains, tears, or pet damage. We also cannot accept items that smell of smoke or other strong odors.


Items we CANNOT accept:


  • Clothing/Linens/Bedding/Drapes/Blinds
  • Baby or Children’s Items
  • Any items that need reupholstered
  • Storm windows/single pane windows
  • Building Materials
  • Organs & Pianos
  • Trampolines
  • Computers, Electronics, etc.
  • Housewares
  • Books
  • Most entertainment centers
  • Commercial Office Furniture & Equipment
  • Storage Headboards, Waterbed Frames, Bedframes missing parts or pieces.
  • Gym Equipment, Weight Benches, BowFlexes and Ski Machines
